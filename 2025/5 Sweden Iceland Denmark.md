## Sweden, Denmark & Iceland (November 2025)

### Day 1: Bengaluru → München → Göteborg (7 Nov)

- **Flight:** Lufthansa (BLR → MUC → GOT).
- Check-in at **Gothia Towers**.
- Visited **World of Volvo** – museum & experience centre (walked from hotel).
- Saw **Liseberg** from outside (closed for winter maintenance).
- Walked to **Haga** district.
- Hot chocolate & _kladdkaka_ at **Café Kringlan**.
- Dinner at **Silvis Palestinska Restaurang** – falafel, tahini, tzatziki, hummus.

---

### Day 2: Göteborg

- Breakfast at **Gothia Towers**.
- **Slottsskogen Park** – penguins, animals, brownies at park cafe.
- Visited **Chalmers University** (met friend Brijesh; mini campus tour).
- Ferry to **Styrsö** island.
- Walk to **Stora Rös** viewpoint.
- Snacks at **HUNDRA bar** (Styrsö).
- Check-in at **Scandic Göteborg** for the night.
- Dinner at **Andrum Café** (vegan).
- Night walk: **Feskekôrka** (Fish Market), city centre, souvenir shopping.
- Used **Västtrafik** day pass for trams & ferries; tried **Ryde** scooters.

---

### Day 3: Göteborg → København

- Morning **FlixBus** to Copenhagen.
- Check-in at **CABINN Copenhagen** (near bus terminal).
- Evening walk: **Rosenborg Slot** gardens & castle exteriors.
- **Strøget** shopping district & Christmas Market.
- Cinnamon bun & hot chocolate at **Espresso House**.

---

### Day 4: København → Reykjavík (Iceland)

- Early flight to **Keflavík** via **Scandinavian Airlines (SAS)**.
- Picked up **Kia Sorento 7-seater** from **LAVA Car Rental** (shuttle pickup).
- **Gunnuhver Hot Springs** & lighthouse viewpoint.
- **Brimketill** lava rock pool & coastline.
- **Kleifarvatn** lake (black sand and mountains around lake).
- **Gígvatnsvatn** volcanic crater lake (Reykjanes Peninsula).
- Check-in at **ALVA Snorrabraut Deluxe Apartment**, Reykjavík.
- Night drive to **Þingvellir National Park** for Northern Lights — faint green hues.

---

### Day 5: Iceland – Reykjavík → Hvolsvöllur

- Morning at **Sky Lagoon** – 7-step ritual, stunning experience.
- Drove to **Hvolsvöllur**.
- Check-in at **Össabær Guesthouse 4** (cabin).
- Saw bright **Northern Lights** from cabin – first clear viewing.

---

### Day 6: South Coast – Towards Vík

- **Skógafoss** waterfall & hike to the top.
- Saw Icelandic horses near **Hverfisvegur**.
- **Dyrhólaey** viewpoint – black sand beach & stone arch.
- Snacks at **Skool Beans Café** (Vík).
- **Reynisfjara** black sand beach & basalt columns at sunset.
- Check-in at **The Barn Hostel**, Vík.
- Witnessed strong **Northern Lights with red auroras** due to geomagnetic storm.

---

### Day 7: Vík → Höfn Region → Vík

- **Yoda Cave** & Viking ship sculpture (Vík Viking Park).
- **Foss á Síðu** & played with a friendly Icelandic dog near **Hamrafoss Café**.
- **Lómagnúpur** scenic mountain stop.
- **Jökulsárlón Glacier Lagoon**.
- Sunset at **Diamond Beach**.
- Long road trip day; returned to **The Barn**.
- Saw mild Northern Lights again.

---

### Day 8: South Coast → Reykjavík

- **Urriðafoss** waterfall on the way back.
- **Kerið** volcanic crater – threw stones on the icy lake for echo sounds.
- **Raufarhólshellir Lava Tunnel** – guided geology tour.
- Check-in at **Einholt Apartments**, Reykjavík.

---

### Day 9: Reykjavík & Golden Circle

- **Þingvellir National Park**.
- **Snorkelling at Silfra** with **DIVE.IS** – between tectonic plates.
- Saw the world’s **oldest Viking parliament** site.
- **Öxarárfoss** waterfall.
- Evening Reykjavík walking tour:
  - **Háteigskirkja** (exterior)
  - **Hallgrímskirkja** (exterior)
  - City centre stroll & souvenirs
- Northern Lights spotting at **Höfði Lighthouse**.

---

### Day 10: Reykjavík → København

- Returned the Kia Sorento at **LAVA Car Rental** (full tank; shuttle to airport).
- Flight to Copenhagen.
- Check-in at **CABINN Copenhagen**.
- Dinner at **Little Italy**.
- Viewed **Tivoli Gardens** from outside.

---

### Day 11: Copenhagen Walking Tour

- **Nyhavn** – colourful harbour photos.
- Famous **Buka Bakery** – pistachio croissant & hot chocolate (worth it!).
- **Christiansborg Palace** (outside).
- **Rosenborg Castle** (outside) – both closed as it was Monday.
- Walked around **Strøget**.
- **Rundetårn** (Round Tower) – viewpoint & cathedral area.
- Lunch at **Falafel Factory**.
- **Kastellet** star-shaped fortress, **St. Alban’s Kirke**, windmill & **Little Mermaid**.
- Used metro & buses for transport.
- Evening **FlixBus** to Göteborg.
- Stayed briefly at friend’s student housing (ÖkStupan).

---

### Day 12–13: Göteborg → Bengaluru

- Flight back from **Göteborg Landvetter** to BLR (via Munich).
- Landed early morning on **18 November**.
- Wonderful memories, long travel days, and stunning photographs.

**Trip Summary:**  
A vibrant Nordic journey blending Scandinavian city culture with Iceland’s dramatic landscapes. From cosy streets of Göteborg and Copenhagen’s festive markets to Iceland’s waterfalls, glaciers, black sand beaches, and multiple Northern Lights displays.

**Total Cost:** ~₹2,31,000  
**Currencies Used:** **SEK**, **DKK**, **ISK**  
**Transport Tips:** Västtrafik for Sweden, Metro & buses for Copenhagen, self-drive for Iceland.

### General Tips & Notes

- Cooked several meals at the Airbnbs in Iceland to save costs. Highly recommended since restaurant food is expensive and many remote places in Iceland have no restaurants.
- For groceries in Iceland, **Krónan** is the best value supermarket (large savings compared to other stores).
- In Denmark (København), **Netto** offers affordable pricing for essentials and snacks.
- In Göteborg, **7-Eleven** is convenient for quick snacks, hot drinks and basic groceries.
